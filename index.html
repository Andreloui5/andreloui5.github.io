About Me

I have a passion for quality design, execution, and customer service.

My name is Craig Gant, and I'm a web developer living in Orlando, FL.

I received my bachelor's degree in music from the University of Nebraska at Omaha and worked full-time in the music industry for over a decade. In addition to performing, I have a great deal of experience in other aspects of the industry— recording, programming, writing, and arranging.

I have over six years of experience in Communications— working as a communications coordinator, professional copywriter, and editor. I am excited by all elements of web development, and especially love to see creative design come to life through code.
